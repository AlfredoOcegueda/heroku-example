COVID-19 Pandemic

How the virus is affecting Mexico.

SARS-COV2 first appeared in China on December of 2019. So far it has become a worldwide pandemic as the disease we call COVID-19. Different countries are applying contingency plans to mitigate the disease in their populations along with the recommendations we all have come to know:

  • Washing your hands frequently.
  • Maintain social distancing.
  • Avoid touching your face.
  • Respiratory hygiene.

The Mexican government, along with CONACYT, have compiled all kinds of data regarding how the disease has affected the populace.

As a team, we wanted to analyze the data given by the government and CONACYT using Python, Javascript, and SQL to visualize how our country is doing with the COVID-19 pandemic.

To accomplish this, we decided to make visualizations that would represent the data we obtained nationally, for each state, and we also thought it was relevant to compare each state. We particularly wanted to compare, between males, females and their specific age group, how many confirmed cases there are in each state.
